About the Subjects

  • +2 Accounting & E-commerce: Explore the principles of accounting and delve into the world of e-commerce.
  • Office Management-II: Build on the skills required for effective office management.
  • Typography-II: Further your knowledge of fonts and typesetting in graphic design.
  • General Foundation Course: A comprehensive course covering multiple subjects to build a strong foundation.
  • On the Job Training: Gain practical experience through real-world job training.
  • General English: Improve your language skills and communication abilities in English.
  • Punjabi (General): Learn the Punjabi language and its cultural significance.
  • EVS: Environmental Studies - Understand the importance of environmental conservation and sustainability.
  • Computer Science: Get introduced to computer science concepts and programming languages.
  • Welcome Life: An orientation course to prepare you for the next phase of life.
