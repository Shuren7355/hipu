About the Subjects

  • Mathematics: Mathematics is the study of numbers, shapes, and patterns. It includes topics such as arithmetic, geometry, algebra, and statistics.
  • Science:Science is the study of the natural world. It covers topics such as biology, chemistry, physics, and environmental science.
  • English (General): English involves the study of language, literature, reading, writing, and communication skills.
  • Geography: Geography is the study of the Earth's physical features, climate patterns, natural resources, and human interactions with the environment.
  • History: History is the study of past events, civilizations, societies, and human achievements.
  • Political Science: Political Science is the study of government systems, political behavior, public policies, and the theory and practice of politics.
  • Economics: Economics is the study of how individuals, businesses, and societies allocate scarce resources to satisfy unlimited wants and needs.
  • Computer Science: Computer Science introduces students to the basics of programming, computational thinking, and problem-solving using computers.
  • Punjabi (General): Punjabi is an Indo-Aryan language primarily spoken in the Punjab region of India and Pakistan.
  • Punjabi (Elective): This is an elective subject in Punjabi that provides further specialization in the language and its literature.
  • Environmental Studies: Environmental Studies is the study of the environment and its interactions with human society.
  • Hindi: Hindi is the national language of India and one of the most widely spoken languages in the world.
  • Welcome Life: An orientation course to prepare you for the next phase of life.
